Lesung Café

Local Taste, Heartwarming Atmosphere

Lesung Café is a culinary business based in Kepanjen, Malang. We serve traditional Indonesian food in a cozy, authentic setting that invites both locals and international visitors to gather.


The name "Lesung" comes from a traditional rice-pounding tool in Javanese culture, symbolizing hard work and togetherness. We embrace this philosophy in every aspect of our café, from food to service.


Our signature dishes like Sego Sambel Lesung, traditional brewed coffee, and local side dishes are more than food—they’re stories from the family kitchen passed down through generations.


Through this platform, we hope Lesung Café becomes known not just as a dining spot, but as a representation of Indonesia’s culinary heritage.
